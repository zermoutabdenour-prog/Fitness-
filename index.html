Phase System
PHASE 1 — WEEKS 1–6: Foundation
Learn movements. Build base conditioning. 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps. Moderate weight. Focus on form and consistency. Calorie deficit begins.
PHASE 2 — WEEKS 7–14: Hypertrophy
4 sets × 8–12 reps. Increase load weekly. Add supersets. HIIT intensity increases. Visible fat loss accelerates.
PHASE 3 — WEEKS 15–20: Athletic Peak
4–5 sets × 5–8 reps heavy. CrossFit-style WODs. Max conditioning. Final body recomposition push.

The Routine
1
Box Breathing Activation
4 sec inhale → 4 sec hold → 4 sec exhale. 10 cycles. Activates nervous system, reduces cortisol. Do this before getting out of bed.
2
Cat-Cow + Thoracic Rotation
10 reps each. On all fours. Opens up the spine after sleep. At your height this is non-negotiable daily.
3
Hip Flexor + Glute Activation
Couch stretch 30s each side + glute bridges 20 reps. Office workers have tight hips — fix this every morning.
4
EMOM Fat Burner — 10 minutes
Every Minute On the Minute for 10 min: Odd minutes = 15 Burpees. Even minutes = 20 Jump Squats. Rest remaining time. Brutal but effective fasted cardio.
5
Hollow Body Hold + Dead Bug
2 × 30s hollow hold + 2 × 10 dead bug each side. Core activation for the day. Essential for posture from desk work.
6
Cold Water Finish + Protein
30s cold shower finish. Then: 40g whey protein + black coffee or green tea. No carbs until training window.

Progression System
STRENGTH Progressive Overload Rules
+
2-Rep Rule
When you complete all sets with 2+ reps in reserve, add weight next session. Upper body: +2.5kg. Lower body: +5kg.
✓
Volume Before Intensity
Weeks 1–6: Master form at 12–15 reps. Weeks 7–14: Drop to 8–12 reps, add weight. Weeks 15–20: Heavy 5–8 reps.
!
Deload Week
Every 4th week: reduce volume by 40%, keep same weight. This is mandatory — skipping deload kills progress.
↑
HIIT Progression
Week 1–2: 20 min. Week 3–4: 25 min. Week 5–6: 30 min. Increase rounds, not just time. Track all WOD times.
RECOVERY When to Push vs Rest
↑
Push harder when:
You slept 7+ hours. HRV is normal. Last session felt manageable. You are in weeks 2, 3, 5, 6 of each phase.
↓
Pull back when:
Joints ache (not muscles). Motivation is zero for 2+ days. Sleep under 6 hours for 3+ days. You are sick.
